## Version 1.0.0

Released: 9 March 2026

This is the first release of the Matching and Weighting site.

It brings together the initial public set of training and reference materials for design-stage causal adjustment in observational evaluation, with a focus on exact matching, coarsened exact matching, and entropy balancing.

### Release Summary

- Main methods report with HTML and PDF output, plus full-code and QA companion pages
- Comparative synthesis across *The Mixtape*, *The Effect*, and Gertler chapter 8
- Companion source-note articles for the three core books and a short propensity-score weighting extension
- Lab section with the LaLonde report walkthrough, Black Politicians, NSW + CPS, and HISP examples
- Concept deck and presenter script for the teaching version of the materials

### Scope Of This First Release

The site is structured around three top-level areas:

- `Articles` for the main report, synthesis, and source-note pages
- `Labs` for guided worked examples and follow-on exercises
- `Slides` for workshop delivery materials
