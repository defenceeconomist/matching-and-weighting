4 QA Decision

The HISP IE in Practice bundle is ready for use.

It follows the book replication do-file closely enough to support teaching, inspection, and reruns in R. The main deviations are implementation-level substitutions where the Stata-specific commands do not have an identical local R equivalent in the current environment.

5 Residual Risks

  1. Matching estimates use MatchIt as the nearest-neighbor engine rather than psmatch2, so very small tie-breaking differences remain possible.
  2. The RDD visuals are analytically aligned but not pixel-identical to the Stata figures that depend on rdplot.
  3. The power-calculation tables are logically aligned with the Stata workflow, but exact numeric parity with sampsi and sampclus is not guaranteed.
